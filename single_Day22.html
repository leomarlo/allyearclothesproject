Day 22

Lviv - the new temporary base

 -  November 13, 2019

Arrived in Lviv this morning. Arrival was deferred, first by some administrative stuff in Vienna and then by a defect carriage. Wanted to get a second hand sewing machine from the Bazaar. Mobile data doesnt work on my phone, even thought I have pimped up my Ukrainian sim card with mobile data. So no Uber, no Yandex, no Uklon. Hmm... Took a ride by taxi, which I ordered from the British Club. I negotiated 100 Hryvnia, but then before I arrived i noticed it at 180 and renegotiated to 150. On the way back I asked a local which bus to take and paid 7 Hryvnia, so less than 5% of the out-journey.

Bazaar was closed when I arrived. So I went to Epicenter to buy a sewing machine for two weeks and return it at the end. Should not be a problem. It's a Leader machine. Not quite the Singer that I had in Georgia, but a Leader.

AllYearClothesProject

I want to create all my clothes for 2020 until the end of the year 2019. But why? And where is the starting point? My starting point is close to zero: I have no plan, no time allocation, no materials, not many contacts in the sewing world and almost no sewing resources … so far. This is not supposed to be a cooking show, where miraculously everything is prepared already. It is instead supposed to be a documented self-experiment. Starting from zero, I wonder how the project evolves and morphs into something and I wonder how I and things around me co-morph with it.
